Overview

My Master's project is titled 'Simulation of Sisyphus cooling of hydrogen atoms for use in the search for dark matter and fifth forces'

The aim of the project is to simulate Sisyphus cooling in hydrogen (and hydrogenic) atom clouds, in order to determine whether it presents a feasible mechanism of supercooling hydrogen.

Zeeman shifts of the 2s and 2p excited states hyperfine structure of Hydrogen.

Zeeman shifts of the ground state hyperfine structure of Hydrogen. States are labelled in the uncoupled basis.
